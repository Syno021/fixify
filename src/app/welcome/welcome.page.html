About Fixify

Fixify is a comprehensive platform that connects Building Managers with Service Providers, streamlining the maintenance and repair process for buildings. Our app offers:

  • Easy posting and management of maintenance requests
  • Access to a network of qualified service providers
  • Real-time notifications and status updates
  • Secure in-app messaging system
  • Ratings and reviews for quality assurance
  • Admin panel for efficient user and role management
